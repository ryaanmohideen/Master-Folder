Teams Spotlight

The Avengers

The Avengers are a team of superheroes brought together to protect Earth from various threats. The team has included many notable heroes such as Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, Hawkeye, and others.

X-Men

The X-Men are a team of mutant superheroes led by Professor Charles Xavier. They are dedicated to protecting mutants and humans alike from threats and discrimination. The team has included many notable members such as Cyclops, Wolverine, Storm, Jean Grey, and others.

Defenders

The Defenders are a team of street-level heroes who protect New York City from various threats. The team includes members such as Daredevil, Jessica Jones, Luke Cage, and Iron Fist.

Sinister Six

The Sinister Six is a group of supervillains who frequently battle Spider-Man. The team has included notable villains such as Doctor Octopus, Electro, Sandman, and others.

The Thunderbolts

The Thunderbolts are a team of reformed villains who work as heroes under the leadership of various characters such as Citizen V, Baron Zemo, and Hawkeye. The team has included notable villains such as Moonstone, Songbird, and others.

The Hand

The Hand is a criminal organization composed of ninja assassins who have frequently battled Daredevil and other heroes. The Hand has also been involved in various criminal activities such as drug trafficking and extortion.

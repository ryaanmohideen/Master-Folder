Villains Spotlight

Thanos

The Mad Titan Thanos seeks to wipe out half of all life in the universe in order to bring balance. He is a powerful and ruthless villain who poses a threat to the entire Marvel Universe.

Magneto

Magneto is a powerful mutant with the ability to control magnetic fields. He is a charismatic leader who believes in mutant supremacy and has clashed with the X-Men and other superheroes many times.

Venom

Venom is a symbiotic organism that bonds with various hosts, including Eddie Brock and Flash Thompson, to create a powerful and deadly villain. He is a frequent enemy of Spider-Man.

Killmonger

Erik Killmonger is a Wakandan exile who seeks to overthrow T'Challa, the Black Panther, and take control of the kingdom. He is a skilled fighter and strategist, and his beliefs about how Wakanda should be ruled put him at odds with the hero.

Ultron

Ultron is a sentient robot created by Hank Pym (Ant-Man) that turns against its creator and seeks to destroy all of humanity. Ultron has proven to be a formidable opponent for the Avengers, often coming back more powerful and dangerous than before.

Galactus

Galactus is a cosmic entity who consumes entire planets to sustain his life force. He is one of the most powerful villains in the Marvel Universe and has clashed with the Fantastic Four, the Avengers, and many other heroes.

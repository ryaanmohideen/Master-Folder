Heroes Spotlight

Iron Man

Billionaire industrialist Tony Stark dons his high-tech suit to become the superhero Iron Man, fighting crime and saving the world from various threats.

Spider-Man

Teenager Peter Parker gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He uses his powers to fight crime in New York City as Spider-Man.

Black Panther

King T'Challa of the fictional African nation of Wakanda, who is also the Black Panther, is a skilled warrior and scientist who uses his advanced technology and fighting skills to protect his people and the world.

Doctor Strange

The brilliant but arrogant surgeon Stephen Strange suffers a career-ending injury and becomes a master of mystical arts, defending the world from supernatural threats as Doctor Strange.

Daredevil

Blind lawyer Matt Murdock uses his enhanced senses to fight crime in Hell's Kitchen, New York City, as the masked vigilante Daredevil.

Wolverine

The mutant Wolverine, also known as Logan, has a powerful healing factor and retractable claws. He uses his abilities to fight for justice and protect his fellow mutants.

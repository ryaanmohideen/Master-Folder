Movies Spotlight

The Avengers (2012)

The Avengers brings together Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye to save the world from an alien invasion led by Loki.

X-Men: Days of Future Past (2014)

X-Men: Days of Future Past follows Wolverine as he travels back in time to prevent a catastrophic event that leads to a dark future for both mutants and humans.

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018)

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse is an animated film that explores the idea of a multiverse, bringing together multiple versions of Spider-Man to save the world.

Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

Thor: Ragnarok sees the God of Thunder teaming up with the Hulk and his brother Loki to save Asgard from the goddess of death, Hela.

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

Avengers: Infinity War brings together the Avengers and the Guardians of the Galaxy to stop the powerful Thanos from obtaining the Infinity Stones and destroying half of all life in the universe.

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

Guardians of the Galaxy follows a group of misfits including Star-Lord, Gamora, Drax, Rocket Raccoon, and Groot as they band together to protect the galaxy from a powerful villain named Ronan.
